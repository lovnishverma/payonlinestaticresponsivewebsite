RBI Guidelines for Secure Online Transactions

Under the latest RBI guidelines, only card issuers and card networks are authorized to store customers' card details. Merchants and payment aggregators must remove stored card data due to previous security risks.

Tokenisation: Starting January 1, 2024, merchants must use "Tokenisation" for transactions, replacing card details with a secure unique code, enhancing safety. UPI already employs tokenisation.

Customer Choice: Customers can opt for tokenisation for easy future payments. Without it, they must enter card details manually each time.

These measures protect financial data and ensure secure digital payments in India's evolving e-commerce landscape.
