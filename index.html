Online Payments

About Online Payments

Online payment allows users to transfer money electronically through various methods such as credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets.

Various Modes of Online Transactions

Credit and Debit Cards

Credit Cards: Customers can make purchases using credit cards and pay later.

Debit Cards: Debit cards deduct the purchase amount directly from the customer’s bank account.

Electronic Wallets

E-Wallets allow customers to make transactions quickly and securely using digital payment platforms like Google Pay, Paytm, and PhonePe.
