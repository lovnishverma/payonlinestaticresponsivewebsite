Online Payments

About Online Payments

Online payment allows you to pay money via the internet. Buyers use this type of payment when purchasing goods online or offline. They can use various online payment methods, including debit/credit cards, wire transfers, net banking, and digital wallets. Online payments are convenient and quick, allowing consumers to pay for subscriptions, clothing, recharges, and more.

The online payment process typically involves four entities: the consumer, merchant, consumer's bank, and merchant's bank. Despite the number of entities involved, the entire process is electronic and usually completed in seconds.
