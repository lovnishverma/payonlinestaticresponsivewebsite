Payment Gateways

A payment gateway is a technology that allows merchants to accept debit or credit card payments securely. It can be a physical device in a store or an online checkout portal.

Key Features

  • Secure transaction processing.
  • Supports multiple payment methods.
  • Ensures encryption and fraud protection.
  • Allows seamless online and offline transactions.

How It Works

Payment gateways securely send customer payment details to the merchant's acquiring bank, ensuring smooth and encrypted transactions.
