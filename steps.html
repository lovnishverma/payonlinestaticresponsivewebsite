Steps in an Online Transaction

The following are the steps involved in an online transaction:

  1. Registration: The consumer has to register online to buy a particular good or service.
  2. Placing an Order: Customers put the desired products in their shopping cart and proceed to payment.
  3. Payment: Various payment options are available like Cash on Delivery, Cheque, Net Banking, and more.
