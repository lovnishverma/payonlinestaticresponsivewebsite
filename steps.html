Steps in an Online Transaction

Below are the key steps involved in a secure and successful online transaction:

  1. Registration: The consumer must create an account to proceed with purchases.
  2. Placing an Order: Customers add items to their cart and proceed to checkout.
  3. Payment: Multiple options like Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, and UPI are available.
  4. Order Confirmation: A receipt is generated with details for tracking.
  5. Product Delivery: The product is shipped to the customer as per the selected shipping method.
  6. Feedback and Review: Customers can review and rate the product and service.
